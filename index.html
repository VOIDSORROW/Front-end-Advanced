About Delmon Group

Delmon Group of Companies is one of the major emergent manufacturing, engineering, trading and marketing group with its Head Quarters strategically based at the Port and Oil city of Eastern Province, Dammam in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

The group is a part of the Al Ghunaim Brothers Holding headed by “Mr. Ahmed S. Al Ghunaim” an impressive record of undertaking major contracts and since then, it has had supplies to various segments in the market and continues to do so.
